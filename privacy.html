Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

PlanCast (“the app”, “we”) is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles.

We do not collect personal data

PlanCast has no account system and does not require sign-in. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information. There are no third-party advertising or analytics trackers in the app.

Data stored on your device

Your plans (title, type, chosen location, date, reminder settings) and app preferences are stored only on your device. They are not uploaded to any server owned by us.

Weather & location lookups

When you search for a place or view a forecast, the place name and its coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve weather data. These requests are used solely to return a forecast and are subject to Open-Meteo’s own terms. We do not attach any identifier to these requests.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server, and no notification content leaves your device.

Purchases

PlanCast Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details. Purchase validation is handled through Apple’s systems.

Children

PlanCast is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
