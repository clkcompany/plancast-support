PlanCast

The weather for your plans, right on time. Plan a trip, picnic or getaway, and get its forecast morning by morning as the day nears.

What it does

Add a plan with a date and place. Choose how many days before to be reminded. From that day on, PlanCast sends you the event day's latest forecast every morning, so you can prepare, pack, or reschedule with confidence. Everything runs on your device; there's no account and no tracking.
